my story

Hi, I’m Wambugu Cindy—a dreamer, a storyteller, and a lover of golden hour light. My journey as a photographer started with a simple gift: a camera for my birthday. From the moment I snapped my first photo, I was hooked. There’s something magical about freezing a fleeting moment and turning it into art that lasts forever.

Photography, for me, is more than just pictures—it’s about capturing the feelings, the laughter, and even the quiet in-between moments that make life beautiful. Whether it’s the soft pinks of sunrise, the electric buzz of city nights, or the peaceful hush of nature, I’m always chasing the light and the stories it reveals.

My style is warm, romantic, and a little bit whimsical. I believe in finding beauty everywhere and sharing it with others. Every session is a new adventure, and I love getting to know the people I photograph—because your story is just as important as the image itself.

why hire me?

Choosing the right photographer is about more than just pretty pictures—it's about finding someone who truly understands you and your vision. Here’s why my clients love working with me:

  • Personal Connection: I take the time to understand your story, your style, and what matters most to you, so every photo feels authentic and personal.
  • Atmospheric Artistry: I specialize in capturing those dreamy, atmospheric moments—think glowing sunsets, sparkling city lights, and moody skies that make your images unforgettable.
  • Relaxed Experience: I know being in front of the camera can feel awkward, so I create a fun, relaxed environment where you can be yourself and enjoy the moment.
  • Attention to Detail: From the lighting to the little candid smiles, I notice the small things that make your story unique.
  • Memories That Last: My goal is to give you more than just photos—I want you to have memories you’ll treasure for years to come.

If you’re looking for someone who will celebrate your story, capture your real moments, and deliver images filled with heart, I’d be honored to work with you. Let’s create something beautiful together!
